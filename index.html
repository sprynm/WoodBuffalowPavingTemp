About us

A local paving team focused on dependable results.

Wood Buffalo Paving serves Fort McMurray and nearby communities with a practical range of asphalt contracting work. We help clients refresh, maintain, or install new asphalt and keep projects moving with clear communication and direct service.

If you need a driveway repaired, a parking lot resurfaced, or a site prepared for new construction, we can assess the base conditions, drainage, and traffic demands before recommending the right path forward.

Our services

Built for homes, commercial properties, and municipal projects.

01

Asphalt paving

New construction, replacements, and resurfacing work designed for the loads and weather that affect northern roads and driveways.

02

Driveways

We look at roots, drainage, old base failure, and installation issues before deciding whether repair, rebuild, or a fresh surface makes sense.

03

Parking lots

Commercial lots are planned around flow, turning paths, load distribution, and the thickness needed to hold up over time.

04

Roads and bid work

We can take on larger bid-based projects and keep the work in-house from start to finish.

05

Resurfacing

A resurfacing layer can restore a sound base at a lower cost than full removal and replacement.

06

Site preparation

Grading, stabilization, drainage, and sub-base prep so the finished surface has a base that can hold up.

Why this matters

A finished surface is only as strong as the base beneath it. We treat drainage, compaction, and sub-grade work as part of the job, not an afterthought.
